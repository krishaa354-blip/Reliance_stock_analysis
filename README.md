# Reliance Stock Data Analysis
This project applies financial data analysis techniques to evaluate the historical performance of Reliance Industries Limited stock. Using statistical analysis and data visualizations, it highlights stock growth, risk, trading patterns, and market trends, helping investors and analysts make informed investment decisions.

The project uses powerful Python libraries such as:
Pandas
NumPy
Matplotlib
Seaborn

The dataset contains historical stock market data including:
Open Price
High Price
Low Price
Close Price
Trading Volume
Date

Features & Visualizations

1. Closing Price Trend (Line Chart)
Displays long-term movement of stock prices.
Helps identify bullish or bearish trends over time.
Insights
Detects upward/downward market direction.
Useful for long-term investment analysis.

3. Candlestick Chart
Shows daily stock movement using:
Open
High
Low
Close prices
Insights
Green candles indicate bullish movement.
Red candles indicate bearish movement.
Helps understand market sentiment and volatility.

4. Monthly Average Closing Price (Bar Chart)
Calculates average monthly closing prices.
Insights
Reveals medium-term trends.
Helps identify seasonal price behavior.

5. Distribution of Daily Returns (Histogram)
Displays frequency distribution of daily returns.
Insights
Measures stock volatility.
Detects extreme gains/losses.
Shows whether returns are stable or highly fluctuating.

6. Monthly Volatility Analysis (Box Plot)
Analyzes spread of daily returns month-wise.
Insights
Wider boxes indicate higher volatility.
Outliers show unusual market movements.

7. Correlation Heatmap
Shows correlation between:
Open
High
Low
Close prices
Insights
Identifies relationships among price components.
Validates market consistency.

8. Volume vs Price Change (Scatter Plot)
Compares trading volume with daily price changes.
Insights
Detects whether major price movements are supported by high trading activity.
Useful for identifying strong market conviction.

9. Cumulative Returns (Area Chart)
Tracks growth of investment over time.
Insights
Visualizes wealth creation.
Highlights drawdown and recovery periods.

10. Moving Average Trend Analysis
Uses:
50-Day Moving Average
200-Day Moving Average
Insights
Identifies long-term and short-term trends.
Detects crossover-based buy/sell signals.

11. Daily Trading Volume Analysis
Displays daily trading activity.
Insights
Higher volume indicates strong investor participation.
Volume spikes may signal important market events.

12. Maximum Drawdown Analysis
Measures decline from historical peak prices.
Insights
Important risk management metric.
Helps investors understand downside risk.
